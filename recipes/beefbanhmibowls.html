Beef Banh Mi Bowls

Description

The traditional Banh Mi sandwich in a bowl! We swap out the bread for rice, and the adding in some yummy sriracha mayo for a little kick.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Yellow Onion
  • Lime
  • Garlic
  • Persian Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sriracha
  • Ground Beef
  • Soy Sauce
